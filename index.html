Welcome to Miftah

A different way to engage with the Quran

Miftah is a study companion for intermediate Arabic students who want to go beyond translation — into grammar, tafsir, personal reflection, and memorisation. Built around the methodology of Ustadh Nouman Ali Khan's 5 Lenses framework from Bayyinah.

"All study passes through a filter that makes it meaningful or renders it useless. That filter is the heart."

The Methodology

5 Lenses to reflect upon the Quran

Each ayah can be explored through five distinct lenses. One tab, one focus at a time.
